A community with a shared story

"Did you receive value?
Pay it forward."

We are not a membership program. Not a subscription. We are a closed community of people who want to be part of a chain that pulls others up.

What it is about

There are people who have gone through something difficult — and emerged stronger. They changed their perspective on themselves, on relationships, on life. And now they feel the need to pass on what they received. Not as advice. Not as a lesson. But as an authentic human story that can show someone else they are not alone.

The "Pay It Forward" club is a place for these people. For those who have chosen the path of conscious change — and want to have others around them who understand. Who live it. Who get it.

No gurus. No "5 steps to a better you". Just people holding each other up on the journey.

"What we have received, we pass on.
That is the whole principle."

What you will find in the club

I.

A Closed Community

People who understand each other. A safe space for sharing challenges, successes, and falls — without masks, without judgment.

II.

Mutual Support

Encouragement, challenges, shared growth. Not advice from "experts", but authentic stories of people who are living it.

III.

Online Lectures

Working with emotions, the inner child, behavioral patterns, raising children, using AI in personal development. Recordings always available.

IV.

Local Gatherings

Invitations to circles and community evenings at the Motivus Space. Real-life contact that no screen can replace.

Who it is for

  • For those who have already undergone inner transformation and want to help others on this journey.
  • For those who are in the midst of change and are looking for people who understand.
  • For those who feel they do not want to be alone in this.
  • For those who believe that sharing experiences changes lives more than any theory.

How it works

Right now, we are building the core of the community in the form of a closed WhatsApp group. We are looking for the first people who want to be there from the beginning — people for whom this idea is not just nice words, but something they truly live.

Initially, we want to gauge the interest in this format, which is why entry is currently completely free (later it will be just a symbolic contribution to operations). If the idea resonates with you and you want to be a part of it, write to us. We will invite you directly to the group where it all begins.

No commitment. No pressure. Just an open door for those who want to walk through.
