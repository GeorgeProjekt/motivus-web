The "Pay It Forward" Club

"Did you receive value? Pay it forward."

Birth of a True Community

The "Pay It Forward" club is the central structural element of the MOTIVUS association. It originated from a clear intention to ensure an organic continuation and spread of personal development among people — without pressure, based on volunteering and lived experience.

This is not a mass-market affair. It is an interconnected ecosystem of individuals who want to step beyond individual self-work and enter a shared format. The community thrives through small contributions, which directly fund the further growth and possibilities of our association.

Membership Architecture

  • Focused Growth: We are primarily focusing on building a strong core in the Moravian-Silesian region, with a reach across the entire Czech Republic.
  • Co-creation: Members are not just passive consumers of services; their contributions shape the future format of retreats and community events.
  • Member Benefits: Exclusive access to men's/women's circles, discounted conditions for transformational coaching, priority booking for retreat events.

Mechanisms of Support

The core idea is highly straightforward: If a personal coaching process, therapy, or retreat with MOTIVUS has helped you, we offer a space to "pass that value forward". Your small regular contribution opens the door to creating a stable foundation for the organization. By doing this, we extract development organizations from their dependency on short-term grants and give them a solid footprint for the years to come.
